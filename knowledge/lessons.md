# Piano Lessons

## Types of Lessons Offered

### 1. Beginner Lessons
- **Foundation building** for new students
- Introduction to piano and music reading
- Proper technique and posture from day one
- Fun, engaging repertoire for motivation
- Building confidence and love for music

**Typical duration**: 30-45 minutes
**Pricing**: From €30/hour

---

### 2. Intermediate Lessons
- **Skill development** for progressing students
- More complex pieces and techniques
- Continued technical refinement
- Introduction to different musical styles
- Preparation for grade examinations

**Typical duration**: 45-60 minutes
**Pricing**: Around €40/hour

---

### 3. Advanced Lessons
- **Complex pieces** and advanced techniques
- Performance-level repertoire
- Advanced theory and analysis
- Artistic interpretation and expression
- Preparing for higher-grade exams or performances

**Typical duration**: 60 minutes or longer
**Pricing**: Up to €50/hour

---

### 4. Exam Preparation
Specialized preparation for:
- **RIAM exams** (all grades)
- **ABRSM exams** (all grades)
- **Junior Certificate Music**
- **Leaving Certificate Music**

Includes:
- Technical work (scales, arpeggios)
- Repertoire selection and preparation
- Sight-reading practice
- Aural training
- Mock examinations
- Exam strategy and confidence building

**Duration**: Customizable, often longer sessions
**Pricing**: Varies based on intensity and requirements

---

## Age Groups Welcome

### All Ages Accepted

- **Children** (extensive experience with young learners)
- **Teenagers** (including exam preparation)
- **Adults** (beginners and returning players)

### Special Note: Children Under 5

Children below 5 years old may be too young for formal piano lessons due to:
- Hand size and physical development
- Attention span
- Fine motor skills

**Assessment available**: Contact us for a consultation if your child is under 5. Some children develop earlier and may be ready.

## Lesson Duration Options

Standard lessons: **30-60 minutes**

Duration is tailored based on:
- **Student's age** - Younger students often benefit from shorter, more frequent lessons
- **Attention span** - Ensuring optimal learning without fatigue
- **Learning goals** - Exam preparation may require longer sessions
- **Skill level** - Advanced students often need more time for complex pieces

### Longer Sessions Available
Extended lessons for:
- Exam preparation (intensive periods)
- College audition coaching
- Professional development
- Performance preparation

## What to Expect in Lessons

### Typical Lesson Structure

1. **Warm-up and review** (5-10 min)
   - Previous week's material
   - Technical exercises

2. **New material** (15-25 min)
   - Learning new pieces
   - Introducing new concepts
   - Skill development

3. **Practice techniques** (10-15 min)
   - How to practice effectively
   - Problem-solving difficult passages
   - Building independence

4. **Theory/Reading** (5-10 min, as needed)
   - Music theory concepts
   - Sight-reading practice
   - Aural skills

5. **Wrap-up** (5 min)
   - Summary of lesson
   - Practice assignments
   - Goals for next week
