## What to Expect

### Initial Contact
1. Reach out with details about your accompaniment needs
2. Provide information about:
   - Type of event (exam, recital, competition, etc.)
   - Music pieces and difficulty level
   - Performance/exam date
   - Your location
3. Receive quote and availability confirmation

### Preparation Process
1. **Music sharing** - Send sheet music in advance (PDF or physical copy)
2. **Schedule rehearsals** - Coordinate 2+ rehearsal sessions
3. **Rehearsals** - Work together on tempo, dynamics, ensemble, interpretation
4. **Final preparation** - Polish and refine for the performance
5. **Performance/Exam** - Professional accompaniment on the day

### What You Need to Provide
- **Sheet music** for the accompanist (in advance)
- **Performance details** - Date, time, venue
- **Any specific requirements** - Exam board guidelines, performance notes, etc.
