## Lesson Details

### How long are lessons?

**30-minute lessons** are recommended for:
- **Young children** - Younger students find it challenging to focus beyond 30 minutes
- **Beginners** - We focus on fundamentals; less is more in the beginning
- Half an hour is the sweet spot for effective learning at these stages

**Note**: If you prefer a full hour for younger students or beginners, we can split it into two 30-minute sessions with a break.

**45-60 minute lessons** are appropriate for:
- Intermediate and advanced students
- Exam preparation
- Those working on complex repertoire

**Extended sessions (beyond 1 hour)** are available for:
- Recital preparation
- College entrance auditions
- Intensive exam preparation

For extended sessions, these are bespoke arrangements. Contact us directly:
- **Phone**: 0857267963
- **Email**: cczcy333@gmail.com
- **WhatsApp**: 0857267963

**See also**: pricing.md for detailed duration and pricing information

### What should I expect in a typical lesson?

A typical lesson includes:
1. Warm-up and review of previous material
2. Learning new pieces or concepts
3. Technical work (scales, exercises)
4. Practice techniques and problem-solving
5. Theory and sight-reading (as needed)
6. Practice assignments for the week

### Do I need a piano at home?

**Yes, a piano or keyboard at home is essential** for:
- Daily practice between lessons
- Reinforcing concepts learned in lessons
- Building muscle memory and technique

**Minimum recommendation**: 61-key keyboard with weighted keys
**Ideal**: Full 88-key acoustic or digital piano

### Which exam board should I choose - RIAM or ABRSM?

**RIAM** is best for:
- Irish students integrating with school system
- National recognition in Ireland
- Irish repertoire focus
- Required for Irish third-level music programs (Grade 8 + Theory)

**ABRSM** is best for:
- International recognition
- Planning to study abroad
- Traditional, comprehensive curriculum
- Commonly required for music college admission worldwide (Grade 8 + Theory)

**Both are equally recognized** by Irish colleges and universities. Grade 8 in either exam board (plus theory) is typically required for music college entrance in Ireland.

**CC can help you decide** based on your goals and circumstances.

## Practice & Progress

### How much should I practice?

**General guidelines**:
- **Beginners**: 15-30 minutes daily
- **Intermediate**: 30-45 minutes daily
- **Advanced**: 45-60+ minutes daily
- **Exam preparation**: Additional focused practice time

**Quality over quantity!** Focused, effective practice is more important than long hours.

### How long until I can play a song?

**Timeline varies** based on:
- Age and prior experience
- Practice consistency
- Complexity of desired pieces

**Typical milestones**:
- **Simple melodies**: 2-4 weeks for absolute beginners
- **Two-hand pieces**: 2-3 months
- **Grade 1 level**: 6-12 months of consistent practice
- **More complex pieces**: 1-2+ years
