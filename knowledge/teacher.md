# Teacher Information - Chenyang Zhao (赵晨阳)

## Background

Chenyang Zhao (CC) was born in Henan, China in 1990. Both of her parents are teachers. She began playing piano at the age of 7, and it has been the centre of her life ever since.

## Education

- **Beijing Normal University** - Graduated with Honours in Piano Performance
- **Beijing Contemporary Music Academy** - Continued studies in Jazz and Popular Music

## Teaching Experience

- **15+ years** of experience working with young students
- Started teaching while still in university through local school and government programs
- Experience in both China and Ireland

### In China
- Prepared students for ABRSM exams
- Prepared students for Chinese piano grades
- Coached students for competitions and recitals

### In Ireland (since 2020)
- Prepares students for RIAM exams
- Prepares students for ABRSM exams
- Teaches Junior Certificate music
- Teaches Leaving Certificate music

## Current Teaching Positions

- **Private tuition** (primary focus) - Kinnegad and surrounding areas
- **Eden School of Music** (Edenderry) - Current teaching position

## Previous Teaching Positions

- **Fermatta School of Music**
- **Piano Lessons Dublin**

## Teaching Style

"My teaching style is joyful, encouraging, and tailored to each student. I love helping children grow their confidence and musical ability, and my students often become fast friends in the process."

### Key Characteristics
- Personalized approach based on individual learning needs and goals
- Focus on building confidence alongside musical ability
- Creates a positive, supportive learning environment
- Develops strong rapport with students
- Adapts teaching methods to suit each student's unique requirements

## Expertise

- All ages welcome (children, teenagers, and adults)
- All skill levels (beginner to advanced)
- Exam preparation specialist
- Piano accompaniment for exams, performances, competitions, and recitals
