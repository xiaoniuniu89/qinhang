# Schedule & Availability

## General Availability

Piano lessons are offered by appointment throughout the week. Specific time slots are coordinated directly with each student based on:

- Student's schedule and commitments
- School hours and homework time
- Family routines
- Travel time between locations

## Scheduling Lessons

### How to Book

1. **Contact us** through the website contact form
2. **Discuss your preferred days/times**
3. **Arrange a trial lesson** at a mutually convenient time
4. **Establish regular lesson time** once committed

### Flexible Scheduling

We work to accommodate:
- School schedules (primary and secondary)
- After-school activities
- Family commitments
- Work schedules (for adult students)

### Regular Weekly Lessons

Most students benefit from **consistent weekly lessons**:
- Same day and time each week (when possible)
- Builds routine and consistency
- Optimal for progress and retention

### Alternative Scheduling

Also available:
- **Bi-weekly lessons** (every two weeks)
- **Intensive periods** (multiple lessons per week during exam preparation)
- **Flexible scheduling** (for irregular schedules, by arrangement)

---

## Typical Teaching Hours

**Note**: These are general guidelines. Actual availability varies by day and is confirmed when booking.

### Weekday Afternoons & Evenings
- **After school hours** are most popular
- Typically between **3:00 PM - 8:00 PM**
- Individual slots confirmed upon booking

### Saturdays
- **Morning and afternoon** slots available
- Typically between **10:00 AM - 4:00 PM**
- Popular for students with busy weekday schedules

### Sundays
- Generally **not available**
- Special arrangements may be possible in exceptional circumstances

---

## Lesson Locations & Scheduling

### In-Home Tuition
- **Flexible scheduling** based on travel route
- Time slots depend on location and proximity to other students
- Coordinated to minimize travel time between lessons

### Eden School of Music, Edenderry
- Lessons scheduled according to school availability
- Fixed time slots at the school location

---

## Holiday & School Break Schedule

### School Holidays
- Lessons typically **follow the school term** schedule
- Breaks during major school holidays (Christmas, Easter, summer)
- **Optional lessons** may be available during breaks by arrangement

### Public Holidays
- No lessons on **Irish public holidays**
- Make-up lessons scheduled when possible

### Summer Schedule
- Reduced schedule during **summer holidays** (July-August)
- Some availability for intensive exam preparation
- Flexible arrangements for students taking summer exams

---

## Booking in Advance

### New Students
- Contact us as early as possible
- Popular time slots fill quickly (especially after-school hours)
- Trial lessons scheduled within 1-2 weeks typically

### Existing Students
- **Regular time slots** are generally maintained term-to-term
- Advance notice appreciated for schedule changes
- Priority given to continuing students for popular times

---

## Cancellations & Rescheduling

### Student Cancellations
- **24-hour notice** preferred for cancellations
- Lessons may be rescheduled when possible
- Consistent cancellations may result in time slot reassignment

### Teacher Cancellations
- Rare, but may occur due to illness or emergency
- Students notified as early as possible
- Make-up lesson always offered

### Weather & Emergency
- Lessons may be canceled for severe weather or emergencies
- Safety is the priority
- Alternative arrangements or online lessons may be offered

---

## Contact for Scheduling

To inquire about current availability or to schedule lessons:

- Use the **website contact form**
- Provide your:
  - Preferred days of the week
  - Time preferences (morning/afternoon/evening)
  - Location (for in-home tuition)
  - Any scheduling constraints

We will respond with current availability and work to find a time that suits your needs.
